# Learning Plans: Week-by-Week Roadmap with 5-Day Study Week

## Overview

This roadmap breaks your 6-month, 24-week (total 168 days) plan into focused learning blocks:

- Study on 5 days each week (Monday to Friday focus)
- 6th day (Saturday) - catch-up, revision, reinforcement, or rest depending on progress
- Sunday as rest or light reflection day (optional)

Effectively:

- 5 study days x 24 weeks = 120 focused learning days
- 24 Saturdays to fix gaps, catch errors, review tough concepts
- Sundays light/non-study days to avoid burnout

---

## Structure

- 6 Months: Month-1 through Month-6
- 24 Weeks: Week-1 through Week-24
- Days:
  - Day-01_plan.md to Day-05_plan.md per week for learning
  - Day-06_plan.md per week for review/correction/revision
  - Day-07_plan.md optional reflection (or skipped)

---

## Incremental "Brick by Brick" Realistic Approach

"I'm thinking of each week as a microservice:

- Days 1-5: Feature development (core skills, projects, problems)
- Day 6: Monitoring & fixes (revision, catch-up, reinforce weak spots)
- Day 7: Maintenance (reflection/planning/rest)"

This keeps pace manageable, reduces burnout risks, and aligns with engineering iterative best practices.
